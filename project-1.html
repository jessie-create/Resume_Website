Economic Development Efficiency in Specific Countries from 1990 to 2012

Introduction

It's an important topic in all countries that saving energy use and reducing pollutant emissions to increase the efficiency of economic development. Many countries try to pursue high efficiency of economic development instead of high increase in GDP. Therefore, in this project, it is mainly to analyze the efficiency of economic development in some specific countries from 1990 to 2012. During the project, I hope to find the causality between GHG emissions and economy by Granger Test. After obtaining the relationship between GHG emissions and economy, I will find the relationship between GHG emissions and economy per capita. Also, the relationships between GHG emissions and different types of gases; GHG emissions and different types of industries will be analyzed by using regression models.
